Ten seconds, one command

Capture is effortless.
Recall is uncanny.

No clipping, no tagging chores, no friction. Save it and move on — the mind does the rest in the background.

~/arxiv-kb — kb 
# save a paper — metadata, LaTeX, PDF, embeddings, all of it
$ kb add 2405.14831
  ✓ ingested HippoRAG: 9 sections embedded (14.2s)

# ask in your own words — meaning, not keywords
$ kb search "how do agents remember across sessions?"
  → Generative Agents · § method · p.4
  → HippoRAG · § method · p.6

# the surprising connections you'd never search for
$ kb spark --kind need_solution
  ✦ future_work in one paper ←→ method in another
